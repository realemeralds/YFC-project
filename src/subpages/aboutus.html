What is DAS?

DAS is a social service organisation which provides a wide array of services for those who struggle with dyslexia. They specialise in the education of many young students who struggle with being able to read, write or spell.

What is YFC?

The YFC competition is a community initiative that aims to provide youth aged between 15 to 25 years old a platform to involve themselves in societal issues. The competition involves the provision of a seed funding to participants, of which participants can use to support their cause.
